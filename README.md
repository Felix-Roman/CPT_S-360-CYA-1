# CPT_S-360-CYA-1
Here is my CYA-1 reflection in which I went to the Idaho National Laboratory event and learned about their many opportunities and experiences they offer.

For this activity, Idaho National Laboratory was hosting an event to students interested in cybersecurity showcasing opportunites to them in their field
and what they had to offer. They went over many things such as their daily life to life acitivites and what to expect when working their/in this field. The thing that left a meaningful impression on me however, was how they talked about their upbringings and how they all differ so much. One great example was of how one of them started out as a janitor and talked about his life. Explaining that no matter what job you start with, as long as you have the drive and willingness to learn that they can help teach you the skills, but the drive is something that can't be taught. When they said this, it made me feel way more invested and enthusiastic since many jobs lately all make it seem like you need to know so much starting out, but here they were really down to earth with you and let you know that some of them didn't even know muchs starting out either, but the drive they showed off let them get to where they are now.
